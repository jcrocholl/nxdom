About NXdom

NXdom is an interactive search engine for available short domain names, created by Johann C. Rocholl.

It's written in about 2500 lines of Python and powered by Django 1.1 on Google App Engine.

The full source code is available at github.com/jcrochol/nxdom under the MIT License.
