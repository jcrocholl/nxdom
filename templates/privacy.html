Privacy Policy

Scoretool protects your privacy. If you use this service, the following information may be stored on our servers:

  1. Your domain name search keywords.
  2. Your session cookie.
  3. Your IP address, to prevent multiple votes for feedback.

We will not use your search keywords or results to register domain names. All of your information listed above is private and will not be shown to other users of the Scoretool service. Only aggregated anonymous information will be published, e.g. monthly top ten search keywords from all users. However, your information is theoretically available to Scoretool and Google engineers because it is stored in the App Engine datastore and request logs.

This policy incorporates the Google App Engine privacy notice:
http://code.google.com/appengine/privacy.html
