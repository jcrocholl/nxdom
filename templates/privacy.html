Privacy Policy

NXdom protects your privacy. If you use this service, the following information may be collected:

  1. Your domain name search keywords (in the request logs).
  2. Cookies for Google Analytics and Google Website Optimizer.
  3. Performance data from your browser (interactive search load time).
  4. Your IP address if you vote or submit feedback, to prevent multiple votes.

NXdom does not collect the domain names that you click on, only their length and top level domain, e.g. "5 letters .com". However, our affiliate partners (Commission Junction and the domain registrars) can see your selected domain name when you check availability.

We will not use your search keywords or results to register domain names. All of your information listed above is private and will not be shown to other users of the NXdom service. Only aggregated anonymous information will be published, e.g. monthly top ten search keywords from all users. However, your information is theoretically available to NXdom and Google engineers because it is stored in the App Engine datastore and request logs.

This policy incorporates the Google App Engine privacy notice:
http://code.google.com/appengine/privacy.html
